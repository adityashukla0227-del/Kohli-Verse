The Journey

LEGACY

From a Delhi boy with a dream to the greatest of his generation.

2008
Debut
International Debut — Sri Lanka
Kohli enters the international arena as a teenager. Raw, hungry, and destined for greatness. Selected for the ODI squad after a stellar U-19 World Cup campaign.
2011
World Cup
ICC World Cup Champion 🏆
India wins the World Cup at Wankhede after 28 years. Kohli scores a crucial 35 vs Pakistan. He escorts MS Dhoni to the crease — an iconic moment in cricket history.
2013
Rise
ICC #1 ODI Batter
Reaches the pinnacle of ODI rankings. His consistency across conditions and pressure situations makes him the most feared batter in world cricket.
2014
Captaincy
Test Captain of India 👑
Takes over Test captaincy from MS Dhoni mid-tour in Australia. Transforms India into a feared Test unit with an aggressive, never-say-die approach.
2016
Peak Form
973 Test Runs — Most in a Calendar Year
The year Kohli was unstoppable. ICC Cricketer of the Year, ICC Test Player of the Year. Destroyed every bowling attack on the planet with surgical precision.
2018
ICC Awards
Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy 🏆
ICC Cricketer of the Year. 2018 saw Kohli score over 2,000 runs across formats. Won every ICC individual award in a single year — something no player has done before.
2023
History
50th ODI Century — Sachin's Record Broken 🎉
Became the first batter to score 50 ODI centuries, surpassing Sachin Tendulkar's record of 49. The Wankhede stood still. India wept tears of joy. The GOAT had spoken.
2024
T20 WC
T20 World Cup Champion 🏆
After years of wait, India wins the T20 World Cup in Barbados. Kohli's 76 in the final seals the trophy. He announces T20I retirement on the pitch, in tears. Legacy complete.
